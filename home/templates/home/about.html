About

FitJacket is your personalized gateway to a healthier lifestyle. Whether you're just getting started or looking to level up your fitness journey, FitJacket empowers users to create accounts, track progress, and receive tailored workout plans. Every user can confidently manage their data and access a customized fitness experience built just for them. It's not just about reps and sets — it's about building habits that stick.

What sets FitJacket apart is its intelligent, user-first design. Users can interact with an LLM chatbot to generate workout plans, discover exercises that match their goals, plan their future workouts, and track their progress. Whether you're a general fitness enthusiast or someone seeking structured coaching, FitJacket adapts to your lifestyle — making fitness feel accessible, purposeful, and inspiring every step of the way.
